#+filetags: :psychology:

* limbic
Network of structures located beneath the cerebral cortex
+ Coordinates:
  1. Feeding (satiety & hunger)
  2. Forgetting (memory)
  3. Fighting (emotional response)
  4. Fornicating (sexual)
