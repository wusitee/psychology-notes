* dream
people all dream, even if we cannot recall our dreams (dreams are reported if awaken during [[id:71ba66f6-9c84-4f09-a9d5-eb1949110e7b][REM]] [[id:8d4cc5a9-20bb-4d80-821d-34fb3fd83153][sleep]])
+ the dreams can organize and consolidate memories or restoring resources.
+ dreams can also occur during: Stage 1-3 (NREM Sleep), but there are just fragments.
* activation synthesis [[id:8b8f2642-cf54-4d93-8566-21f475c94ba1][theory]]
Dreams reflect brain activation originating in the pons, followed by efforts of the cerebral cortex to weave these inputs into a story
+ damage to the forebrain can eliminate dreaming, even when the [[id:38d62b18-a405-45a7-a342-879d9f11065f][pons]] is intact.
