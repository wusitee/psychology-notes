** sensation
physiological process
the process of transduction
receive information and convert to [[id:7e97ccb9-3a1e-4d5f-8d21-92d25d096922][neural impulse]]
** perception
Psychological  process
the process of making sense
same sensation may lead to different [[id:d0e080a9-5876-49b5-94b6-ebedeeea2ad8][perception]].
