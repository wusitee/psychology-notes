#+title: motor development
#+filetags: :psychology:

* from gross to fine motor coordination
Through daily play and movement, babies and toddlers begin to develop fundamental motor skills
+ Gross motor skills (big movements like crawling, walking, and jumping)
+ fine motor skills (smaller actions, like grasping and picking up tiny objects)
